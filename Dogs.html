Types Of Dogs

Labrador Retriever:

Friendly, outgoing, and high-energy. Excellent family pets and good with children.

German Shepherd:

Intelligent, loyal, and versatile. Often used in police and military roles.

Golden Retriever:

Gentle, friendly, and tolerant. Great for families and known for their affectionate nature.

Bulldog:

Calm, courageous, and friendly. Known for their distinctive wrinkled face and pushed-in nose.

Beagle:

Curious, friendly, and merry. Small to medium-sized hounds with a keen sense of smell.

Poodle:

Intelligent, active, and trainable. Comes in three sizes (standard, miniature, and toy) and known for their hypoallergenic coat.
